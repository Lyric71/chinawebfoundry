Yes. The team is in mainland China. We test on local networks, not through a VPN from somewhere else. We handle Chinese hosting providers and government portals in Mandarin, file ICP paperwork ourselves, and when something goes sideways at 2 a.m. Beijing time we're in the same timezone dealing with it.

We've seen what happens when agencies claim China expertise but operate from London or Singapore. Things work until there's a real issue, then debugging stalls because they're testing from outside the firewall. We don't have that problem.
