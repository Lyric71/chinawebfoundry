---
question: "How long until our WordPress site is actually live in China?"
order: 3
category: "process"
---

Four to eight weeks for most projects. Week one is the audit. Weeks two through four cover the ICP filing, mostly waiting. Server setup and migration happen during weeks three to five. Plugin swaps and performance tuning fill weeks four through six. Testing and launch wraps around weeks six to eight. If you need the commercial ICP licence rather than the standard Bei'an, add a couple of weeks. We overlap phases where possible but the ICP wait can't be sped up.
