question: "Can you integrate WeChat Pay and Alipay into our WordPress site?"

category: "technical"

Yes, and you should. We hook up both as payment gateways, working with WooCommerce or custom checkout setups. Beyond payments we connect WeChat Official Accounts for social login, set up Open Graph tags for proper sharing inside WeChat, and link Mini Programs where it makes sense. WeChat isn't just a chat app - it's the operating system of daily life for most Chinese consumers. If your site doesn't play nice with it, you're losing people before they consider buying.
