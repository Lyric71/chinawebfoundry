Baiduspider is bad at rendering JavaScript, so server-side rendering is mandatory for proper indexation. Baidu still uses the meta keywords tag. Sites with ICP licenses on mainland servers get a measurable ranking boost. Sitemaps and indexing go through Baidu Ziyuan, a completely separate ecosystem from Google Search Console. Backlink value skews toward Chinese-language domains (.cn, .com.cn). Duplicate content gets penalised harder than on Google. You cannot just adapt your Google SEO strategy - the whole approach needs rebuilding from the ground up.
