---
question: "Why doesn't my WordPress website work in China?"
order: 1
category: "technical"
---

The Great Firewall. China's national filtering system blocks or throttles most Western services WordPress depends on - Google Fonts, Google Analytics, reCAPTCHA, YouTube, Facebook pixels, major CDN providers. Sites that load in under two seconds from New York can take 15 to 20 seconds from Shanghai, or just timeout entirely. If nobody has done China-specific work on your WordPress setup, your site is basically invisible to anyone in mainland China.
