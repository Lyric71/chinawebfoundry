---
question: "What exactly is an ICP license? Do I actually need one?"
order: 2
category: "technical"
---

Yes, you need one - no clever workaround exists. ICP (Internet Content Provider) is a permit from China's Ministry of Industry and Information Technology, required by law for any website hosted on a mainland server. Two types: ICP Bei'an (备案) for informational sites, and ICP Jing Ying (经营许可证) for sites processing transactions. Without the Bei'an, no Chinese hosting company will serve your domain. We manage the whole filing, typically two to four weeks.
