---
question: "Your team is in China. How do we handle the time difference?"
order: 4
category: "process"
---

Our project managers speak English and Chinese (a couple speak French too). We overlap with European hours in the morning and pick up North American teams in our evening. Sprint cycles with weekly updates in your inbox as the baseline. Tools are whatever you already use - Slack, Teams, Zoom, email. We've managed projects with teams across Paris, Chicago, and Sydney simultaneously. Timezone has never been the thing that caused problems.
