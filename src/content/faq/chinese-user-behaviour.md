---
question: "How do Chinese internet users behave differently on websites?"
order: 14
category: "process"
---

Over 80 percent of web traffic in China is mobile. QR codes are everywhere, sharing happens through WeChat, payments go through Alipay or WeChat Pay. Chinese web design tends to be significantly more information-dense than Western users expect.

Trust signals are different too. Chinese visitors look for the ICP number in the footer, real company registration info, and customer service on WeChat. An email contact form doesn't communicate "legitimate business" the way it might in the West. If the right trust markers aren't visible, people leave.
