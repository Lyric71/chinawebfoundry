question: "Which WordPress plugins break in China, and what do you use instead?"
order: 5
category: "technical"
---

Anything calling a Google API: Google Fonts, Google Maps, reCAPTCHA, Analytics. Same for Facebook SDK, Twitter embeds, and anything on a blocked CDN. Replacements: Baidu Tongji for analytics, Baidu Maps, Tencent Captcha for bot protection, self-hosted fonts, and Alibaba Cloud CDN or Tencent CDN for delivery. Before any migration we audit every plugin on your site and produce a report: stays, goes, or gets replaced - and with what.
