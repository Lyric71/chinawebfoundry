Speed improvement hits first. A site going from 15-second loads to under three in China sees bounce rates drop 40 to 60 percent. Conversions follow, though specifics depend on your product, price point, and audience. The strategic payoff is Baidu indexing - once set up correctly, you're reaching organic traffic that literally didn't exist for you before. Clients in competitive verticals typically see solid Chinese search traffic within three to six months. Less competitive niches, faster.
