A major media organisation needed their content accessible in China without maintaining a separate site. We audited every external dependency, replaced Google Fonts, Analytics, and Maps with local equivalents, built a dual-stack hosting architecture with geo-routing, and launched a single WordPress instance serving both global and Chinese audiences. Content teams continued using the same CMS workflow with no disruption.
