A global manufacturing company needed their extensive WordPress site to work in China. The existing site took 18 seconds to load from the mainland, with multiple resources blocked by the Great Firewall. We migrated all 2,000 pages onto ICP-compliant hosting in mainland China, replaced every blocked dependency, and configured a dual-stack CDN for global and Chinese audiences. Load times dropped to under 2 seconds with zero downtime during the transition.
