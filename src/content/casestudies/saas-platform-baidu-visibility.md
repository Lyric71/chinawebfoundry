A B2B SaaS platform had a WordPress site that was completely invisible to Baidu. The JavaScript-heavy frontend couldn't be crawled, the sitemap wasn't configured for Baidu Spider, and there was no Chinese-language content. We rebuilt the frontend for server-side rendering, created a Chinese content strategy targeting high-intent Baidu keywords, and submitted through Baidu Webmaster Tools. The site went from zero indexed pages to over 400 within three months.
