An international e-commerce brand wanted to sell directly to Chinese consumers. Their existing WooCommerce setup only supported Visa and Mastercard, had no Baidu visibility, and was hosted overseas. We rebuilt the checkout with Alipay and WeChat Pay, optimised the entire product catalogue for Baidu, secured ICP licensing, and moved hosting to mainland China. Revenue from Chinese customers tripled within six months of launch.
