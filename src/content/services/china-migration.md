## What's included

Migration to China is more than copying files to a new server. We replicate your entire WordPress environment on China-compliant infrastructure, replace blocked dependencies, and configure DNS for optimal routing.

## Our process

### 1. Audit and planning
We map every component of your current setup - themes, plugins, custom code, databases, and media.

### 2. ICP licence application
We guide you through the ICP licence process, preparing all documentation and liaising with the hosting provider on your behalf.

### 3. Parallel environment setup
We build a staging environment on Alibaba Cloud while your current site stays live.

### 4. Data migration and testing
Database, media, and configuration are migrated. We test thoroughly from multiple Chinese cities.

### 5. DNS cutover and go-live
We switch DNS with minimal downtime, verify everything works, and monitor for 48 hours post-launch.

## Who this is for

This service is for companies that already have a WordPress website hosted outside China and need it to perform inside the Great Firewall.
