## What's included

End-to-end migration of your WordPress site to China-compliant infrastructure. We handle every technical and regulatory step so your existing site stays live until the new environment is fully tested and ready.

## Key deliverables

- **ICP licence application** - complete management of the licensing process
- **Server provisioning** - China-based hosting setup on Alibaba Cloud or Tencent Cloud
- **Database migration** - full transfer of content, media, and configuration
- **DNS cutover planning** - zero-downtime switch with rollback strategy
- **Plugin replacement** - swap blocked plugins for China-compatible alternatives
- **Post-migration testing** - performance verification from multiple Chinese cities

## Who this is for

Companies with a live WordPress site that needs to be moved onto Chinese servers with full compliance and no disruption to the existing global site.
