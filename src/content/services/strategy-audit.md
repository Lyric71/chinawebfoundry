## What's included

A comprehensive analysis of your existing WordPress site against every factor that affects performance, compliance, and visibility in mainland China.

## Key deliverables

- **Great Firewall scan** - identify every blocked resource, script, and external dependency
- **Performance benchmarking** - load time testing from multiple Chinese cities
- **Plugin compatibility audit** - flag plugins that rely on blocked services
- **ICP licence assessment** - determine licensing requirements and timeline
- **Baidu indexability review** - check crawlability and technical SEO readiness
- **Prioritised roadmap** - clear action plan ranked by impact and effort

## Who this is for

Companies with an existing WordPress site that want to understand exactly what it takes to make it work in China before committing to a full migration.
