## What's included

We analyse your existing WordPress site from a China-first perspective, testing load times from multiple Chinese cities, identifying blocked resources, and mapping every dependency that won't work behind the Great Firewall.

## Our process

### 1. Discovery call
We learn about your business goals in China, your existing infrastructure, and your timeline.

### 2. Technical audit
Our engineers test your site from inside China - measuring load times, identifying blocked scripts, fonts, and APIs.

### 3. Compliance review
We assess your ICP licence eligibility and map out the application process for your entity type.

### 4. Roadmap delivery
You receive a prioritised action plan with clear costs, timelines, and expected performance improvements.

## Who this is for

This service is for companies that are considering entering the Chinese market or are experiencing poor website performance in China and need a clear plan of action.
