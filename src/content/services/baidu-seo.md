## What's included

Baidu uses different ranking signals than Google. We optimise your WordPress site specifically for Baidu - from technical setup to content structure to backlink strategy.

## Key deliverables

- **Baidu Webmaster Tools** setup and site verification
- **Technical SEO** - sitemap submission, meta tags, structured data for Baidu
- **Content optimisation** - keyword research using Baidu's tools, content structuring
- **Performance monitoring** - monthly ranking reports and traffic analysis
- **ICP licence verification** - Baidu prioritises sites with valid ICP licences

## Who this is for

Companies that need their WordPress site to be discoverable by Chinese users searching on Baidu.
