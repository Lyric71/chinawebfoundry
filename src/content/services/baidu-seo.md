## What's included

Baidu uses different ranking signals than Google. We optimise your WordPress site specifically for Baidu, covering technical setup, content structure, keyword research, on-page optimisation, and authority building within China's web ecosystem.

## Key deliverables

- **Baidu Webmaster Tools** setup and site verification
- **Technical SEO** - sitemap submission, Baidu-specific meta tags, mobile usability, page speed from inside China
- **Chinese keyword research** - native research using Baidu Index and Baidu Keyword Planner, not translated Google keywords
- **On-page optimisation** - title tags, headers, internal linking, and content structured for Baidu's crawler
- **Authority building** - backlinks and presence on Chinese platforms including Baidu Baike, Baidu Zhidao, WeChat, and Weibo
- **Ongoing management** - monthly ranking reports, algorithm change response, competitor monitoring

## Who this is for

Companies with a WordPress site in China that is not generating organic traffic. Businesses launching a new site for the Chinese market. Marketing teams looking to reduce dependence on paid acquisition in China. SEO professionals who handle Google expertly but need a China-based team for the Baidu side.
