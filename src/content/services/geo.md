## What's included

GEO is the work of becoming the source AI quotes when somebody asks a question. Five engines run inside the Great Firewall, each playing by different rules. We work all five: DeepSeek, Doubao, Kimi, Yuanbao, and Baidu AI.

## Key deliverables

- **Visibility diagnosis** - 80 to 150 query matrix run on real devices in Shanghai, Beijing, and Shenzhen across all five Chinese AI platforms. Citation rate, brand recommendation rank, sentiment score.
- **Source-tier audit** - mapping every claim on your site against verifiable evidence (T1) versus marketing copy without proof (T3), then rebuilding the weak spots.
- **Content cluster engineering** - pillar pieces backed by FAQs, comparison guides, scenario walkthroughs, and case studies that hold up from any angle.
- **Multi-platform distribution** - syndication into Baijiahao, Zhihu, industry portals, and where it makes sense, formal Baidu Baike entries.
- **Technical groundwork** - JSON-LD structured data, robots.txt for Baiduspider, Sogouspider, ByteSpider, XML sitemaps, canonical tags that don't split authority across language versions.
- **Continuous monitoring** - weekly tracking and adjustments. DeepSeek shipped four major revisions in 2025 alone. What worked in February stops working by May.
