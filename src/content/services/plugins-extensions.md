## What's included

Many popular WordPress plugins depend on external services blocked in China - Google Fonts, reCAPTCHA, external CDNs, and analytics scripts. We identify every broken dependency and replace it with a working alternative.

## Our process

### 1. Plugin audit
We test every active plugin from inside China and catalogue which ones fail, degrade, or slow the site.

### 2. Replacement mapping
For each broken plugin, we identify a China-compatible alternative or build a custom solution.

### 3. Implementation
We install, configure, and test replacements on a staging environment before deploying to production.

## Who this is for

Companies migrating an existing WordPress site to China that rely on plugins with external dependencies.
