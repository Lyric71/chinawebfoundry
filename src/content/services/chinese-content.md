## What's included

We create, adapt, and manage native Chinese content for your WordPress site. Optimised for Baidu, culturally relevant, and published within your CMS.

## Key deliverables

- **Content strategy** - keyword research using Baidu Index and Baidu Keyword Planner, competitor analysis, editorial calendar planning around key Chinese commercial dates
- **Native copywriting** - original Chinese content by professional native speakers, written for Baidu ranking and Chinese reading habits
- **Transcreation** - brand message adaptation that preserves intent while rebuilding for cultural relevance in China
- **WordPress content management** - multilingual workflows, Gutenberg configuration for Chinese publishing, content migration and team training

## Who this is for

Companies launching a Chinese-language WordPress site, businesses with stale translated content that Baidu has stopped ranking, marketing teams planning campaigns for Chinese consumers, and e-commerce teams needing product pages written properly for the Chinese market.
