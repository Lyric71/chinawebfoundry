## What's included

We create and localise content in Simplified Chinese that resonates with your target audience. This goes beyond translation - we adapt tone, cultural references, and structure for Chinese readers and Baidu's ranking algorithms.

### 1. Content audit
We review your existing English content and identify what needs translation, adaptation, or creation from scratch.

### 2. Localisation
Native Chinese writers adapt your content, preserving brand voice while ensuring cultural relevance.

### 3. Baidu optimisation
We structure content with Baidu SEO best practices - keyword placement, meta tags, and structured data.

### 4. Implementation
Content is published in WordPress with proper bilingual setup and hreflang tags.
