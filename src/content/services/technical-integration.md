## What's included

We integrate your WordPress site with the Chinese digital ecosystem - payment gateways, social platforms, mapping services, and analytics tools that actually work behind the Great Firewall.

## Key integrations

- **WeChat** - login, sharing, mini-programme links
- **Alipay** - payment processing
- **Baidu Maps** - replacing Google Maps
- **Baidu Tongji** - analytics replacing Google Analytics
- **Tencent Captcha** - replacing Google reCAPTCHA

## Who this is for

Companies that need their WordPress site to interact with the Chinese platform ecosystem beyond basic content display.
